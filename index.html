CIVIL-TEC · UIST
01 — Engineering Technology
🏗️ Civil Engineering

Diploma in Civil Engineering at UIST prepares students for Bangladesh's booming construction and infrastructure sector. The 4-year program covers structural design, construction management, and modern surveying techniques under BTEB curriculum.

Program Overview

Civil Engineering Technology at UIST is a 4-year (8 semester) Diploma program affiliated with Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB). Students gain hands-on skills in construction, structural analysis, road and highway design, water supply, sanitation, and project management.

The program blends theoretical knowledge with extensive practical lab work and industry visits. Graduates are equipped to work as sub-assistant engineers, site supervisors, and construction technologists in government and private sectors.

ELEC-TEC · UIST
02 — Engineering Technology
⚡ Electrical Engineering

Diploma in Electrical Engineering at UIST covers power generation, distribution, industrial automation, and electronics — producing job-ready technologists for Bangladesh's energy and manufacturing sectors.

Program Overview

The Electrical Engineering Technology program is a comprehensive 4-year BTEB-affiliated diploma that trains students in power systems, electrical installation, PLC-based automation, electronics, and renewable energy systems. The program has strong emphasis on practical skills through fully equipped electrical labs.

Graduates are competent in industrial wiring, motor control, transformer maintenance, and smart grid technologies — making them highly sought-after by factories, utilities, and construction firms across Bangladesh.

MECH-TEC · UIST
03 — Engineering Technology
⚙️ Mechanical Engineering

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering at UIST trains students in machine design, manufacturing, thermodynamics, and modern CAD/CAM technologies — ideal for Bangladesh's growing industrial and manufacturing sectors.

Program Overview

The Mechanical Engineering Technology diploma is a rigorous 4-year BTEB program encompassing machine design, workshop technology, fluid mechanics, thermodynamics, and CNC/CAD-CAM operations. Students receive intensive hands-on training in the institute's fully equipped mechanical workshop.

The curriculum is designed to meet the demands of Bangladesh's manufacturing, garment machinery, shipbuilding, and engineering industries. Graduates can work as mechanical technologists, production supervisors, and maintenance engineers.

Diploma in Computer Science & Technology at UIST is the most in-demand program — covering software development, networking, database management, cybersecurity, and modern AI fundamentals aligned with Industry 4.0.

Computer Science & Technology (CST) is a 4-year BTEB-affiliated diploma program at UIST that provides comprehensive training in programming, web development, database systems, computer networking, and emerging technologies. It is the highest-demand technology at UIST with extensive lab facilities.

🚗 Automobile Engineering

Diploma in Automobile Engineering at UIST prepares students for the rapidly evolving automotive industry — from conventional vehicle mechanics to modern EV technology, engine diagnostics, and automotive electronics.

Automobile Engineering Technology is a 4-year BTEB diploma program that covers the full spectrum of automotive systems — engine technology, transmission, braking systems, automotive electronics, and the growing electric vehicle (EV) sector. With Bangladesh's rapidly growing transport sector and the global shift toward electric vehicles, automobile engineers are increasingly in demand in service workshops, transport companies, and manufacturing facilities.

TEXT-TEC · UIST
06 — Engineering Technology
🧵 Textile Engineering

Diploma in Textile Engineering at UIST directly serves Bangladesh's largest industry — the RMG sector. Students learn fabric technology, garment production, quality control, and textile machinery maintenance for immediate industry placement.

Program Overview

Textile Engineering Technology is a specialized 4-year BTEB diploma program tailored for Bangladesh's Ready-Made Garment (RMG) and textile industries. The program covers the complete textile production chain — from fiber to finished fabric — including spinning, weaving, dyeing, finishing, and garment manufacturing.

UIST's strong industrial linkages with major RMG factories ensure direct job placement. The program includes regular industry visits and factory attachment programs, giving students real-world exposure before graduation.
